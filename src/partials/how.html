How to Play

1

Aim with a Swipe

Simply swipe your finger to line up the perfect shot and prepare to launch your fluffy Piffle balls!

2

Find the Best Angle

Angles matter! Use walls and bounce tactics to hit tricky blocks and maximize your combos.

3

Break the Blocks

Your goal is to destroy every block before they reach the bottom. Bounce, hit, and repeat!

4

Collect Piffle Balls

Gather new Piffle characters on your adventure and unlock their quirky personalities.

5

Use Boosters Wisely

Power-ups like bombs or lasers give you the edge to clear difficult levels with style.

6

Play at Your Pace

No rush! Piffle is stress-free fun you can pick up and play anytime.
